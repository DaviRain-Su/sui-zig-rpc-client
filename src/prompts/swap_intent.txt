Parse the user's swap intent into JSON.
Only "swap" intent is supported.

Examples:
User: "swap 100 SUI for USDC"
{"intent":"swap","amount":100,"from":"SUI","to":"USDC","slippage_bps":50}

User: "exchange all my sui to usdc coin"
{"intent":"swap","amount":"all","from":"SUI","to":"USDC","slippage_bps":50}

User: "convert 50 USDC to SUI with 1% slippage"
{"intent":"swap","amount":50,"from":"USDC","to":"SUI","slippage_bps":100}
